Data Privacy Statement

In accordance with current data protection regulations, Eurac Research, as Data Controller (hereinafter the Controller), hereby provides you (the Data Subject) with information about how your data is processed. All personal data is dealt with in compliance with EU Regulation No. 2016/679 and the Italian Data Protection Code, Legislative Decree No. 196/2003.

1. Purpose and legal basis for the Processing:

Any and all personal data that is in the possession of the Controller, or that may be requested by the Controller, is necessary to register as a user with the Eurac Research CLARIN Centre (ERCC) and will only be used for the purposes of identifying the user and checking their authorization when using the services provided by the ERCC. The legal basis for the processing of your personal data for the purposes indicated above is your consent to the processing of your personal data. You can withdraw the consent at any time by contacting the data controller.

2. Obligatory or voluntary nature of providing the requested data and possible consequences of failure to provide such data

The provision of your personal data is voluntary, but any refusal to provide such data will not allow the correct use of the services.

3. Methods of processing

Your personal data will be recorded and stored in electronic format. This knowledge and storage of personal data of the Data Subject is necessary in order to process it according to the above-stated purposes. All changes to such data should be immediately communicated to Eurac Research in order to ensure the data's correct processing.

Data concerned:

The ERCC uses a federated login. This means that your Identity Provider (your university or research institution) that you use to log in with the ERCC will automatically provide some data about you. Of this we will save your e-mail address (so we will be able to contact you if needed) and a unique identifier, which might also be the e-mail address or something different, e.g. your username at your institution. We also save which institution you are affiliated with as this is part of the unique identifier.

The ERCC services uses cookies and the tracking plugin Matomo to gather statistics about the use of our services. These data will be anonymized automatically.

4. Information on the retention period of personal data:

Your personal data will be stored for the time period necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

5. Recipients of the data processed and transfer of data:

The recipients of the data are the persons in charge of data processing activities, authorized and instructed by the data controller for data processing activities.

Your data will not be communicated to and processed by third parties.

6. Transfer of data:

Your personal data will not be transferred to any country or international organization outside the European Union.

7. Existence of automated decision-making process

There are no automated decision-making processes.

9.The Data Subject's Rights:

At any time, the Data Subject has the right to request from the controller access to and rectification or erasure of personal data or restriction of processing as well as the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Where the processing is based on the consent, the Data Subject has the right to withdraw consent at any time. The Data Subject may also exercise all other rights pursuant to current data protection regulations (art. 7, Italian Data Protection Code and art. 13, paragraph 2, b), d) and 15-21, EU Regulation No. 2016/679) by writing an email to privacy@eurac.edu.

You may cancel your registration at any time and revoke your consent to the storage of your data. You will then no longer be able to use those ERCC services that require login. Revocation of consent to the registration constitutes a revocation of consent for tracking your data; separated revocations are not possible.
